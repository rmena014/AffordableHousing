About Us

RCRH is a non -profit home organization in the Carolinas and we offers low-cost mortgages and down payment assistance for qualified home buyers whose needs are not met by the market. We finances the development of housing for people with special needs and provide foreclosure prevention assistance and access to housing counseling services. When working with CRRH you work with integrity and professionalism of our staff, management and partners. Here we do what we say we will do and manage resources wisely. There are quarterly meetings so our donors know how and where their hard earned money is going.


Thank you for visiting our page and we hope to have inspired you in becoming a donor today or in the near future.
